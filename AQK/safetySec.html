Safety & Security

At AdventureQuest Kids, your child's safety is our top priority. Every aspect of our program has been designed to ensure a safe, engaging, and memorable experience for all participants. From our strict LARP safety protocols to our highly trained staff, we’ve implemented comprehensive safety measures to give you peace of mind.

LARP Safety Protocols

We take every precaution to ensure that all LARP activities are safe for every participant. Our foam weaponry is specifically designed for safe, soft-contact use, and every weapon is inspected before each adventure. Here’s how we make sure combat and non-combat roles are equally safe:

  • Foam Weapon Checks: All foam swords, shields, and spell balls are thoroughly checked for any damage before each use. We ensure that the materials are soft, flexible, and safe for children of all ages.
  • Combat Safety Guidelines: During combat scenarios, children are instructed on proper fighting techniques that emphasize controlled, light strikes. There is no striking above the shoulders or at sensitive areas like the groin. Children are trained to respect each other and abide by the rules of engagement.
  • Non-Combat Roles: For children who prefer not to engage in combat, we offer roles like medics, strategists, or messengers. These roles allow children to fully participate in the adventure while avoiding direct physical activity.
  • Supervised Combat: All combat activities are supervised by staff members who ensure that combat remains safe, fun, and non-aggressive.

Medical & First Aid Readiness

All staff members at AdventureQuest Kids are trained in basic first aid and CPR to handle any medical situation that may arise during our adventures. We prioritize preparedness to ensure your child’s well-being throughout the day. Here’s what we have in place:

  • First Aid Training: Each member of our staff undergoes comprehensive first-aid training. Our team is equipped to handle common injuries like cuts, scrapes, dehydration, and other minor injuries that could occur during outdoor play.
  • First Aid Kits: We carry fully stocked first-aid kits with us on every adventure, ensuring that medical supplies are always nearby. These kits include bandages, antiseptic wipes, gauze, adhesive tape, and other essential supplies.
  • Emergency Procedures: In the rare event of a medical emergency, our staff is trained to respond quickly and effectively. We have direct communication access to local emergency services, and our first-aid responders can stabilize the child while awaiting professional medical assistance.
  • Parent Notification: In case of any medical incident, parents will be immediately notified with details on how the situation was handled and any necessary follow-up actions.

Staff Qualifications

Our staff members are highly trained in outdoor education, child supervision, and LARP combat techniques. We take great care in selecting individuals who have a genuine passion for teaching and engaging with children. Here’s an overview of the qualifications our staff hold:

  • CPR Certification: All staff members are certified in CPR, ensuring they are ready to respond to cardiac emergencies.
  • Outdoor Education Expertise: Many of our staff members have backgrounds in environmental science, outdoor education, and wilderness survival. This ensures that our adventures are not only safe but also educational and enriching.
  • Child Supervision Training: Every staff member undergoes rigorous training on child supervision, including conflict resolution, positive reinforcement, and behavioral management techniques.
  • LARP and Martial Arts Experience: Our combat instructors have significant experience in safe foam weapon combat and martial arts, ensuring that every combat encounter is carefully choreographed for fun and safety.

Supervision Policies

We maintain a strict staff-to-child ratio to ensure that every participant is well-supervised and safe during the adventure. Our staff members are strategically placed throughout the group to provide constant supervision and assistance when needed.

  • Staff-to-Child Ratio: Our standard ratio is 1 staff member for every 5-7 children. This allows us to provide individual attention and keep a close watch on the group at all times.
  • Role of Lead Guide (Sage Oracle): The Lead Guide oversees the entire adventure, ensuring that all safety protocols are followed and that the group remains cohesive and on track. They are supported by Rangers (Assistant Guides) and Battle NPCs who help manage both combat and non-combat participants.
  • Regular Check-Ins: Staff perform regular check-ins with each child to ensure they are feeling comfortable, hydrated, and safe. Any issues are addressed immediately to maintain the safety and enjoyment of the group.

Child Privacy Policy

Your child’s privacy is important to us. We have implemented strict privacy policies to ensure that your child’s personal information and media are protected.

  • Photography & Media Releases: We only take photos or videos of participants with the explicit consent of their parents or guardians. Any media that is shared on our website or social media platforms will be pre-approved by parents.
  • Data Protection: We collect only the necessary information to process bookings and ensure the safety of your child. This data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your consent.
  • Parental Access: Parents have the right to review and request the deletion of any personal information we have collected about their child.

If you have any concerns about your child’s privacy, please don’t hesitate to contact us, and we will be happy to address any questions you may have.
