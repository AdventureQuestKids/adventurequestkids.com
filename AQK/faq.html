Frequently Asked Questions

What should kids bring to the adventure?

  • Comfortable hiking shoes
  • Sunscreen and a hat
  • A water bottle
  • Weather-appropriate clothing

What’s provided by AdventureQuest Kids?

We provide snacks, all necessary LARPing gear, and materials for educational activities.

How long does the adventure last?

Each adventure lasts about 3 hours, including breaks.

How does AdventureQuest Kids ensure safety?

Safety is our top priority. All of our foam weapons are regularly inspected, and staff is trained in first aid.

What happens in case of an emergency?

We have emergency procedures in place and carry mobile phones to contact emergency services if necessary.

Are the staff qualified?

Our staff are outdoor educators and certified in child safety and first aid.

How do I book an adventure?

You can book through our online form. We’ll confirm via email.

Do you offer group rates?

Yes, we offer group rates for schools, scout groups, and parties.

What’s your refund policy?

Cancellations made 7 days in advance are eligible for a full refund. Late cancellations get a 50% refund.

Are the adventures accessible for children with physical or dietary needs?

Yes, we work with parents to accommodate any physical or dietary needs. Please inform us in advance.

Are there non-combat roles for children who prefer not to engage in battles?

Yes, non-combat roles such as strategists or medics are available for kids who prefer a different role.

What happens if it rains or there's extreme heat?

If there are unsafe weather conditions, we will reschedule the adventure. You can choose to reschedule or receive a full refund.
