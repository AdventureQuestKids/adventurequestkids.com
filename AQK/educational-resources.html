LARP Combat & History

LARPing (Live Action Role-Playing) brings medieval combat to life in a fun and safe way. Learn the basics of LARP combat and explore its ties to real historical events and martial techniques!

  • Foam Weapons: All combat in our adventures is performed with specially designed foam weapons to ensure safety. Children learn how to safely block, parry, and strike during their LARP combat training.
  • Medieval Combat History: Many LARP techniques are based on real medieval martial arts. Historical weapons such as swords, shields, and spears are represented in our adventures.
  • Combat Roles: Children can choose to be Knights, Wizards, or other adventurers, each with different combat roles and abilities.

Learn more about LARP combat and history from Amtgard, a leading organization in live-action role-playing.

Environmental Stewardship Tips

At AdventureQuest Kids, we believe in teaching the next generation how to protect and preserve the environment. Here are some easy ways for kids to help take care of the natural world:

  • Leave No Trace: When you go on an adventure, always pack out what you pack in! Picking up litter and leaving nature as you found it helps keep our deserts clean and healthy.
  • Water Conservation: In the desert, water is precious. Help conserve water by turning off the tap when brushing your teeth and taking shorter showers.
  • Respect Wildlife: Observe animals from a distance, and never disturb their habitats. Every creature has a role in the ecosystem, and we should respect their space.
  • Plant Native Plants: Native plants are adapted to the desert environment and need less water. Planting them in your yard can help conserve water and provide a habitat for local wildlife.

Learn more about Leave No Trace principles and how you can help preserve nature for future generations.
